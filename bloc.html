Bloc

Recycling or recycling is an act of the utmost importance for society since it involves the reuse of different types of elements and objects that would otherwise be discarded, contributing to the formation of more garbage and, ultimately, damaging continuously to the planet. Recycling, as a process of recovering and transforming materials to later convert them back into raw material, has many benefits, of which the collection and classification of a large amount of materials that are scattered in urban and tourist areas stands out. By recycling we contribute to preserving the ecosystems present in our urban and natural environments. Additionally, it unites us in the common goal of preserving the environment.
